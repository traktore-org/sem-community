# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in SEM a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, hardware setup, or background.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in issues and discussions
- Sharing hardware test results and configuration tips
- Helping other users troubleshoot their setups
- Accepting feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to SEM

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and community forums.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/traktore-org/sem-community/issues). All complaints will be reviewed. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
